Welcome to War Bot!

War Bot is a grand strategy war game I have been building since 2023. It is command-based, run through a discord bot where you invade countries, forge alliances, and develop economies. The bot recieves frequent updates and has a dedicated player base.
This website contains details of everything about War Bot, from gameplay to maps to a record of past games. Feel free to use the navigation bar at the top right to learn more about the bot.

Update 1.6.1 - Centralization
July 11, 2026

Centralization has been added to War Bot! At the cost of 100 political power, you can now centralize your country, prioritizing growth in your capitals and territories with financial centers, at the expense of other cities!

Also bundled in this update is the addition of -unitmap, which displays a map of all of your units of a specific type! More information can be found in Detailed Updates!
