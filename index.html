Welcome to War Bot!

War Bot is a grand strategy war game I have been building since 2023. It is command-based, run through a discord bot where you invade countries, forge alliances, and develop economies. The bot recieves frequent updates and has a dedicated player base.
This website contains details of everything about War Bot, from gameplay to maps to a record of past games. Feel free to use the navigation bar at the top right to learn more about the bot.

Newest Update
Update 1.5.3 - The Ground Warfare Update

This update is a major overhaul of the pre-existing ground warfare system. It adds two new types of units, Cavalry and Mechanized Infantry, and splits the former generic Tank into Light Tanks and Heavy Tanks. Each of these units has distinct advantages, whether it's being fuel-efficient, cost-efficient, or attack-effective. It also adds entrenchment, which is a setting you can apply to any ground troop that increases its defense stat by 1.5x.
There are a lot more things this update adds, such as support for multinational rail, which was requested to strengthen partnerships, or the extensible admin & player settings.
More about this update can be viewed in Detailed Updates
