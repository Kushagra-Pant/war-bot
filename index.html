Welcome to War Bot!

This is a passion project I have been building since 2023. It is a command-based strategy game run by a discord bot where you invade countries, forge alliances, and develop economies. This web page contains details of everything to do with the bot. Feel free to use the navigation bar at the top right to learn more about the bot.

Latest Update - Financial Centers!
Update 1.4.5

The most recent updates adds Financial Centers. Financial Centers are buildings that double territorial tax revenue, and halve it's -upgrade and -setcapital prices. However, while useful, they are very pricey - at $120 000, they are the most expensive item in the game. As such, they are most strategic when used for the long-term.
