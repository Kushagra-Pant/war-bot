Welcome to War Bot!

This is a passion project I have been building since 2023. It is a command-based game run by a discord bot where you invade countries, forge alliances, and develop economies. This web page contains a nice summary of everything to do with the bot. Feel free to use the navigation bar at the top right to learn more about the bot.

Latest Update - Statues!
Update 1.4.2

The latest update adds Statues - they boost your stability, but at a price. They cost $50,000 to build and provie 0.05 stability per tax (0.1 per hour). However, this stability boost only adds to you - if an enemy takes the territory with your statue, they start losing stability! So a statue acts as a buff for you, and a debuff for enemies that seize them. You can view all your statues with -statues
Additionally, a new command, -bulldoze was added. For the price of $20,000, you can bulldoze any building in one of your territories. Previously, you had to scorch your territory to remove all buildings, and now with the potential for a negative building (an enemy statue) in your land, this new feature will surely be useful!
Finally, a new cosmetic command but a great addition nonetheless is... -popmap! This is a heatmap of the territories with the largest populations! You can find a demo in How to Play > Detailed.
